History

Rafting has a rich history that dates back to ancient times when people used simple inflatable boats made from animal skins to navigate rivers. Over the centuries, rafting evolved into a popular recreational activity, with modern rafts made from durable materials like rubber and PVC. Today, rafting is enjoyed worldwide, offering thrilling adventures on various rivers.
